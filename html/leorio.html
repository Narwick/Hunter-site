Leorio Paradinight

Leorio Paradinight (レオリオ゠パラディナイト, Reorio Paradinaito) is a Rookie Hunter and a member of the Zodiacs with the codename "Boar" (亥い, I). He is currently a medical student, with a goal to become a doctor.
